I am assistant professor at the Department of Engineering Physics of the École Polytechnique Montréal where I hold an MEI Research Chair in Quantum Photonics and lead the Polyquantique Research Group. I am also affiliated with two Regroupements stratégiques funded by [https://frq.gouv.qc.ca/en/](FRQNT), [https://www.intriq.org](INTRIQ) and [https://coplweb.ca/](COPL). 

I completed my [https://academictree.org/physics/tree.php?pid=164839](my PhD) thesis on [https://tspace.library.utoronto.ca/handle/1807/71623](Very Nonlinear Quantum Optics) at the [www.physics.utoronto.ca](University of Toronto). While in Toronto I was also a [http://vanier.gc.ca/en/scholar_search-chercheur_recherche_2012.html](Vanier) and [http://www.osa.org/en-us/foundation/programs/boris_p_stoicheff_memorial_scholarship/](Stoicheff) Scholar and a Junior Fellow of [https://www.masseycollege.ca](Massey College).
